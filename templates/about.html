What We Offer

Our tool provides the following features to enhance your YouTube video experience:

  • Transcript Extraction: Quickly extract transcripts from YouTube videos in multiple languages, including Hindi and English.
  • PDF Downloads: Once the transcript is retrieved, you can download it as a PDF for easy offline reference or sharing.
  • Keyword Highlighting: Our system highlights important keywords from the transcript, allowing you to get more insights on specific terms by linking them to further resources.
  • Summary Generation: Need a quick overview? We generate concise summaries of the video content to save you time.
